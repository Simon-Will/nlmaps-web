Frequently Asked Questions

How do I activate annotation mode?

First, register and log in. Then click on your name to reach your profile and click on “Edit Profile”. Here you can activate the annotation mode.

How much do I need to vary my place and area names?

Since it’s very difficult for a human to come up with lots of different place names, you don’t need to focus on this. It’s alright if you reuse your locations and I will diversify the reused ones automatically.

Is the order of the two locations important in queries of clas “Dist Between”?

Yes! Please put the location that is first in the NL query also as the first location in the MRL query.

The system misspells my locations!

Yes, that happens sometimes, unfortunately. I hope it doesn’t occur too often.
